Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Cor3 Solutions collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information in compliance with the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012 (R.A. 10173).

Effective: January 1, 2025  ·  Last Updated: February 2025

Section 01

Who We Are

Cor3 Solutions ("we", "us", "our") is a Philippines-based digital agency providing web development & machine learning, admin task management systems, social media management, and UGC content services.

For all privacy-related matters, contact our Data Protection Officer at:

Section 02

Information We Collect

We only collect information that is necessary to respond to your inquiries and deliver our services.

Information you provide directly:

  • Full name, email address, and message content via the contact form
  • Service interest and project details you share with us
  • Communications via email, phone, or social media

Technical data collected automatically (minimal):

  • Browser type and version
  • General geographic region (country level)
  • Pages visited and approximate session duration
  • Referring URL

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising pixels, third-party analytics (e.g. Google Analytics), or collect any sensitive personal data (financial details, government IDs, health information).

Section 03

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and project quote requests
  • Deliver and manage the services you engage us for
  • Send service-related communications (project updates, invoices)
  • Improve our website and service quality

We will never sell, rent, trade, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Section 04

Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not share your personal data except in these limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: Trusted tools used to process contact form submissions (e.g. FormSubmit). These providers act only on our behalf under confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal compliance: We may disclose data if required by Philippine law or a valid legal order.
  • Business transfer: In the unlikely event of a merger or acquisition, data may transfer as part of that transaction with advance notice to you.
Section 05

Data Retention

We retain contact form submissions and client communication records for up to 3 years after your last interaction with us, or as required by applicable law.

You may request deletion of your data at any time. We will action all deletion requests within 30 days, subject to any legal retention obligations.

Section 06

Your Rights Under R.A. 10173 (Data Privacy Act of 2012)

As a data subject, you have the right to:

  • Be informed — know what personal data we hold about you and how it is processed
  • Access — request a copy of your personal data we hold
  • Rectification — request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure / blocking — request deletion or suspension of processing your data
  • Object — object to processing of your personal data under certain conditions
  • Data portability — receive your data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format
  • Damages — be indemnified for damages sustained due to inaccurate, incomplete, or unauthorised use of your data
  • File a complaint — lodge a complaint with the National Privacy Commission (NPC)

To exercise any of these rights, email us at Cor3Solutions@gmail.com. We will respond within 15 business days.

Section 07

Data Security

We implement the following technical and organisational safeguards to protect your data:

  • HTTPS — all pages served over TLS encryption
  • Content Security Policy (CSP) — restricts which scripts and resources may load
  • URL allowlist validation — all navigations checked against an explicit allowlist before redirect
  • Input sanitisation — all user-supplied text escaped before DOM insertion
  • Form validation — client-side checks on all form fields before submission
  • noopener enforcement — all external links opened in new tabs include rel="noopener noreferrer"
  • Limited access — data access restricted to authorised personnel only

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we are committed to protecting your information using industry best practices.

Section 08

External Links

Our website may contain links to external platforms (e.g. social media profiles). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and recommend reviewing their own privacy policies.

All external links on this site are validated against an allowlist before any browser redirect occurs. If you encounter an unexpected redirect, please report it to us immediately.

Section 09

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe we have inadvertently received data from a minor, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Section 10

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of our website after any update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Questions About This Policy?

If you have any questions, concerns, or wish to exercise your data rights, please reach out to us directly.
